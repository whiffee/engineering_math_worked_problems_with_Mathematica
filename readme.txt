engineering_math_worked_problems_with_Mathematica

Exercises from the final edition of a popular engineering math text. A collection of 1332 mostly odd-numbered exercises. Worked at a mediocre skill level in Mathematica 10. Browse PDFs on the locally hosted site, then come back and download anything interesting.
